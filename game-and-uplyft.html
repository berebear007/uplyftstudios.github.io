JOIN THE TOURNAMENT

Listen up Texas! This event is an unprecedented hybrid of gaming, music, culture, and giving back coordinated by Uplyft Studios. We are creating an eSports tournament that benefits 40 nonprofits to make a long lasting impact on the community. All ages are welcome to compete, attend and have a good time, whether you're a fan of eSports, a philanthropist, or a Fortnite aficionado. This event has all the elements needed for you to experience an epic weekend in Houston.

This event is part of an initiative called Dollar and A Game. A portion of all proceeds made from the event will go towards the creation of game development centers in economically disadvantaged areas in the Houston Metropolitan area. Scholarships will be awarded to five featured students that volunteered for the event since its inception. They will be recipients of a scholarship fund totaling $20,000 each. This will allow them to move forward in life with experience and additional funding towards their continued education.
